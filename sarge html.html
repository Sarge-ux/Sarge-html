Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine everything while pasta is hot.
